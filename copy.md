## FAQ

**Can you make a spare key if I have one working key?**
> Yes. If you have at least one working key, we can create a spare key for your vehicle. This includes cutting the blade and programming the transponder/remote functions.

**Do you offer non-destructive entry?**
> Yes. We use professional techniques and tools to unlock vehicles without causing damage. Non-destructive entry is always our first approach.

**What diagnostics can you do?**
> We provide full vehicle diagnostics using professional equipment. This includes reading and clearing fault codes, viewing live data, checking systems, performing service resets, and explaining findings clearly.

**Do you clear fault codes?**
> Yes. We can read and clear fault codes. We'll also explain what the codes mean and advise whether further action is needed.

**How fast can you arrive?**
> Response times depend on your location and our current schedule. When you get in touch, we'll confirm our estimated arrival time. We aim to be prompt and will keep you updated.

**Do I need to come to you?**
> No — we're a mobile service. We come to your location, whether that's home, work, or roadside across London.
